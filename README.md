## Overview

This repository contains the complete research work, source code, processed data products, and results associated with my M.Tech thesis on Bayesian inference constraints on the faint-end slope of Fast Radio Burst (FRB) energy distributions.

The analysis is based on the HECATE FRB catalogue and employs Bayesian statistical techniques and Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) sampling to estimate key population parameters.

---

## Research Objectives

* Constrain the faint-end slope (γ) of the FRB energy function.
* Model FRB population properties using Bayesian inference.
* Compare inferred parameters with observational data.
* Study implications for FRB progenitor populations.
